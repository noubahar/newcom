FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What services do we offer?

Cake Agency is an industry leading ecommerce agency and Shopify partner, we offer a whole range of services to support ecommerce brands big and small.

We’re a full service ecommerce agency, which means we can empower brands no matter what their needs might be. Our primary services are Shopify development, ecommerce marketing, and branding & design. Outside of these primary services, we also offer general ecommerce consultancy, mobile app development for ecommerce brands, Shopify migrations, and long-term strategic support.

What industries do we specialise in?

Our background as retailers gives us a leg up when it comes to building fashion and footwear brands. However, in our decade plus of trading as an agency, we’ve worked with all different kinds of brands and ecommerce businesses. From construction equipment to consumer packaged goods, watches to wine, and motorcycles to make-up - there’s no industry we can’t work with.

Our team of experts has deep expertise and experience in understanding the unique challenges for each industry, allowing us to deliver tailored solutions that drive results. Whether you're a startup or an established, enterprise-level business, we have the knowledge and tools to support your goals.

What brands do we work with?

Where do we start!

Some of the biggest brands we work with include international travel brand It Luggage, lifestyle fashion brand P&Co, sleepwear brand Cyberjammies, alternative fashion brand Killstar, and menswear brand LUKE 1977.

Additionally, we’ve previously worked with international brands such as Bad Monday, &Sons Trading, TentBox, and Verve. All of these brands have benefitted from our expertise across the whole mix of ecommerce.

Who are our partners?

We’re partnered with a number of different platforms across the ecommerce and marketing mix.

Primarily, we’re a Shopify Plus Partner, this means we’re recognised by Shopify, as one of the best agencies at building and maintaining Shopify stores.

We’re also partnered with a number of different technology platforms that further enable ecommerce brands. Some of the main ones include, Klaviyo, Loyalty Lion, Recharge, Reveni, AirWallex, and Klevu.

Which platforms do we specialise in?

As a Shopify Plus partner our primary platform specialism is building and optimising Shopify websites. Our Shopify specialism knows no bounds, from working on Shopify theme builds to custom Shopify development for enterprise-level ecommerce brands trading on Shopify Plus - we can do it all. Taking it a step further, we have a unique specialism in helping brands move to Shopify from other platforms.

From a marketing perspective, we’re best positioned to help brands on their performance marketing with our specialism in Meta advertising and empower them with their CRM and Email marketing with our Klaviyo expertise.

What ecommerce marketing services do we offer?

Our primary ecommerce marketing services are performance marketing, search engine optimisation, CRM & email marketing, loyalty marketing, and our bespoke Gross Demand Plan product.

In addition to these core services, we have also helped brands with merchandising, influencer marketing, written content creation, affiliate marketing, and our ecommerce empowerment Shopify apps.

Do we do one-off projects alongside long-term partnerships?

Yes!

Whilst we find working with brands for multiple years to be the most rewarding kind of partnership, we’re more than happy to work with brands no matter what their needs might be.

Regardless of if your brand is only looking for assistance migrating to Shopify from another ecommerce platform, is looking for a brand or website uplift, or needs guidance with running an individual marketing campaign, we’d be more than happy to help you with your project.

Some of our best work has been on individual projects. Our Shopify Migration for Equiport, our website uplift for Roarsome, and our work with Birmingham Museums, have all been one off projects.

Do we only work with brands based in the UK?

No, of course not

Whilst we are based in the UK ourselves, this does not mean that we exclusively work with UK businesses. In fact, some of our most successful projects have been with brands based overseas. We’ve previously worked with brands based in America, Europe, The UAE, and more.

Whilst we may not be the first choice for brands based outside of the UK due to language, timezone, and market restrictions, we’re more than welcome to working with brands no matter where they might be based.

Where are we located?

We are proudly located in Birmingham’s Jewellery Quarter. Our business has a strong history in the UK’s second city, and some of our longest and most successful partnerships are with brands who also call it their home.
